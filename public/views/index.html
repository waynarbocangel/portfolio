About Me

Hello I'm Waynar Bocangel Calderon!

I'm a third year B.S Computer Engineering student at the University of California San Diego. I moved from my homecountry Bolivia to the United States in 2019 to pursue a career in software engineering, and have been involved in some amazing projects since then.

Learning about the industry and working with brilliant people has pushed me to appreciate the creative and innovative side of software engineering. I love design and user centric projects that have ideas with a heart and a mission at their core. I believe technology has the power to improve our day-to-day lives whether through education, music, or lifestyle.
